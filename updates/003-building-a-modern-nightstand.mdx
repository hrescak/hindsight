This is a beginner-friendly woodworking guide for building a [simple modern nightstand](/updates/002-finding-a-narrow-nightstand). It's limited to basic tools and materials, and it doesn't break the bank.

<img src="/updates/003_01_photo.png" alt="Finished nightstand" height={574} />

## Instructions

Cut the panels to size
Make pocket holes
Cut rabeds on the back
Cut grommet holes
Assemble

---

Cut drawer to size
cut drawer front 45%
make rabbeds
Assemble

---

Finish with poly
FInish drawer front
Put legs on
Put grommets on
